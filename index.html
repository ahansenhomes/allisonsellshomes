Allison's approach

The strategy that gets multiple offers

1

Price it right with data

Allison pulls 12–24 months of comparable sales and analyzes active competition. Strategic pricing attracts more buyers and creates urgency — not a long listing that sits.

2

Stage and photograph professionally

Staged homes sell for more and sell faster. She advises on every detail — from decluttering and paint touch-ups to professional photography with drone footage and floor plans.

3

"Coming soon" marketing

Allison markets your home as "coming soon" for 1–2 weeks before showings begin. Buyers see the photos and floor plan — but can't schedule yet — which builds anticipation and urgency.

4

Weekend launch with open house

Showings begin Friday. She typically runs a Friday evening or Saturday open house to maximize traffic in the first weekend — the most critical period for generating multiple offers.

5

Negotiate the best deal

With multiple offers on the table, Allison keeps all buyers informed and competing — improving price, terms, and conditions. She aims for the best total package, not just the highest number.
